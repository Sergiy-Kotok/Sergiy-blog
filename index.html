    Max Korzh

    He was born on November 23, 1988 in Lunynets, Brest region.
    He graduated from a music school in the piano class. At the age of 14, he became interested in hip-hop.
    He recorded his first song, "Dad Luninets", at the age of 17.
    By that time, Max and his friends had created their own group, Lunclan, whose members rapped in Belarusian.
    The group recorded the Internet album "Talk to me in Belarusian",
    and in February 2007 joined the Minsk underground party M.U.Skool.
